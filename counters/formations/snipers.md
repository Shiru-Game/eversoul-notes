# Enemy Snipers Formation

## Enemy Goal
- Sneak pass the front and snipe the rear souls or highest ATK souls.
- They are either dealing damage or inflicting debuffs like stun/silence/sleep/charm to disable the DPS.

## Enemy Common Souls
- `Mephistopheles`
- `Jade`
- `Manon`
- `Naiah`
- `Ayame`
- `Weiss`
